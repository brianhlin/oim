Thank you for registering the ##SC_NAME## Support Center. Your registration will be added to the OSG Support Structure.

As a registered support center you should have a representative attend the weekly OSG Operations Meeting to review issues facing your users and sites. Details can be found at http://twiki.grid.iu.edu/bin/view/Operations/WebHome

There are several mailing lists associated with the OSG, as a new SC you may want to subscribe to the following mailing lists:

osg@opensciencegrid.org  -- For general OSG announcements
osg-general@opensciencegrid.org  -- For general technical discussion and open community support
osg-operations@opensciencegrid.org  -- Operations activities
osg-ops-status@opensciencegrid.org  -- OSG Status Reports

Details for subscribing to these lists and a list of other OSG mailing lists you may be interested in subscribing to can be found at http://www.opensciencegrid.org/Consortium_Mailing_Lists.

Please contact the Grid Operations Center (GOC) at goc@opensciencegrid.org if you have any questions.

Thank you for registering your support center, and we look forward to your participation supporting the OSG!
